Scientist Action and Advocacy Network

We are a New York-based group of scientists who partner with partisan and non-partisan organizations that are creating positive social change.

William T. Adler

Will is the founder of ScAAN and a Predoctoral Fellow in the Center for Neural Science at NYU. He is passionate about bridging the gap between scientists and organizations that could use pro bono scientific labor and expertise.

His doctoral research focuses on confidence.

Dr. Silvia Lopez-Guzman, MD

Silvia is a Predoctoral Fellow at the Center for Neural Science at NYU. She is interested in supporting advocacy groups fighting against juvenile incarceration, racial profiling, and the criminalization of drug addiction. She is working on an initiative to connect public defenders with scientific expert witnesses.

Her research focuses on the connection between drug addiction and decision-making.

Jenn Laura Lee

Jenn is a Predoctoral Fellow at the Center for Neural Science. She is spearheading ScAAN's project with Raise the Age NY, providing scientific support for legislation that would raise the age at which juveniles (16 and 17 year-olds) are tried as adults in New York State.
